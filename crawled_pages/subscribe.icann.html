Sign Up for ICANN News Subscriptions

Receive the latest ICANN news directly to your inbox!

When new content is added or updated on https://www.icann.org, ICANN will send you a daily or weekly email with links to the content. You will receive the daily news email Tuesday through Saturday between 12:10am - 12:40am UTC and the weekly news email on Saturday between 01:10am - 01:40am UTC.

Simply select the topics you are most interested in and the frequency youd like to receive the ICANN email alerts, and youre set.

To subscribe, sign in to your ICANN Account.

If you dont have an ICANN Account, you need to create an account.

Once you create your account, you will see a Welcome to Your ICANN Account landing page. From there, select the ICANN News Subscriptions tile and then you will be able to select your content and frequency preferences.
